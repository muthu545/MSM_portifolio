Selected projects

pipeline to outcome, start to end
01
Immune resilience signature — Aging Cell 2025
Built the pipeline and ran the analysis that produced the immune resilience signature, using the Framingham Heart Study cohort (approximately 2,306 participants). Found a 15-year survival difference between optimal and extreme immune-resilience-degraded groups. Took the project from pipeline design through to publication.
Related disease-association findings from the same paper, each tied to its own cohort: cardiovascular disease risk in the same Framingham Heart Study cohort; metabolic syndrome association in a separate cohort; and obesity evaluated independently in both the COVID-19 patient cohort and the metabolic syndrome cohort.
Aging Cell 2025 Best Paper Prize
02
Epithelial barrier function in allergic rhinoconjunctivitis — JACI 2017
Led the transcriptomics pipeline analyzing epithelial barrier function and inflammation in response to house dust mite challenge, from pipeline build through to published finding.
JACI 2017
03
COVID-19 patient sample collection & Shiny monitoring dashboard
Coordinated the collection and storage of 10,000+ COVID-19 patient samples with medical teams, pathology lab staff, and outpatient nursing. Built a Shiny dashboard on de-identified data to support physician treatment and triage decisions during the pandemic — a standalone project distinct from the Nature Communications 2023 paper.
10,000+ samples Shiny dashboard
04
Signature-score pipeline validated across public datasets
Built and tested a signature-score pipeline across public datasets (GEO, ArrayExpress, dbGaP). The validated pipeline fed preliminary data into four DoD-funded projects evaluating elite human performance in military personnel: trauma, heat stress, exercise, and spatial disorientation.
GEO / ArrayExpress / dbGaP 4 DoD projects
05
HIV transcriptomics pipeline — VA Center grant
Built the HIV transcriptomics pipeline that provided preliminary data used to help secure a VA Center grant. The PNAS 2015 CCR5/HIV epigenetics paper is one downstream output of this larger pipeline effort.
VA Center grant PNAS 2015 (partial)
06
59th Medical Wing (DoD/USAF) Exercise grant — sample operations
Led sample collection, informed consent, HIPAA form logs, and the participant roster/master list for the Exercise grant, coordinating internal UTHSCSA stakeholders and external USAF personnel.
DoD / USAF
